Our Story

We are a team of ambitious first-year BSIT students who created TM2R DIGIHEALTH. Our goal was to build a clean, reliable, and easy-to-navigate resource for health information, using data sourced directly from the World Health Organization. This project represents our passion for technology and our desire to make a positive impact. Meet the team that made it happen.
